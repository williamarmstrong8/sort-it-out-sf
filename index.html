About Sort It Out

Sort It Out is a San Francisco-based professional organizing service dedicated to transforming homes and workspaces into beautifully functional environments. We believe that organization is not about perfection, but about creating space for what matters most to you.

Our team brings a thoughtful, practical approach to every project, using our proven 3 'S' System, Sort, Select, Store, to help you conquer clutter and reclaim your peace of mind. Whether you need a single room refreshed or a whole-home transformation, we tailor our services to your unique needs, routines, and style.
